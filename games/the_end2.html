WELCOME TO THE END!!!

A brutal, unforgiving survival game. Dodge a constant, apocalyptic rain of meteorites until your inevitable, final impact. Test your reflexes against the cosmos. There are no heroes, only survivors. Death is the only end.

Controls:

  • **Jump (Max 2):** W, Up Arrow, or tap top half of the screen.
  • **Move Left:** A, Left Arrow, or tap lower-left quadrant.
  • **Move Right:** D, Right Arrow, or tap lower-right quadrant.

Difficulty & Obstacles

Difficulty

Controls fireball speed and platform/fireball generation.

**Easy:** Slow meteorites.

**Medium:** 2.3x speed rapid meteorites.

**Hard:** 5x speed intense meteorites rain.

Super Fireball

Enables SUPER FIREBALLS, be warned game could be unplayable for most at this state!!!.

Ground Obstacles

Enables random map earthquakes to throw off your control.
